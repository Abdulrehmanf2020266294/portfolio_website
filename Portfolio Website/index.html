Minar-e-Pakistan

Minar-e-Pakistan is a national monument located in Iqbal Park in Lahore, Pakistan. It was built to commemorate the Lahore Resolution of 1940, which eventually led to the creation of Pakistan.

Wazir Khan Mosque is a historic mosque located in the city of Lahore, Pakistan. It was built in the 17th century during the reign of Emperor Shah Jahan. The mosque is renowned for its stunning Mughal architecture.

Bahria Eiffel Tower

Bahria Eiffel Tower refers to a landmark located in the Bahria Town housing project in Karachi, Pakistan. It is a replica of the Eiffel Tower in Paris and serves as an iconic structure within the Bahria Town community.

Fairy Meadows is a breathtaking destination located in the Gilgit-Baltistan region of Pakistan, near the base of the Nanga Parbat, the world's ninth-highest mountain
